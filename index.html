Frequently asked questions

How is Keyword Tokenizer useful?

This tool can help you with on-page optimization. You will know which specific words are the most important to include in the title, headings, and the rest of the content based on the keyword research.

Where can I get volumes of my keywords?

Popular keyword research tools such as Ahrefs, Semrush or Marketing Miner can help you identify relevant keywords and their average monthly search volume in a chosen country (or globally). Alternatively, you can use Google Search Console, which shows impressions of your queries.

Can I find out how often individual words appear in the data set?

Keyword Tokenizer does not currently support this feature, although it may be implemented in the future. In the meantime, you can use an N-grams tool.
